    • About Us

    Bridging the gap between theory and application.

    We are a youth- led organization that exists to bridge the growing divide between academia and the real world. We are a member ship that spans across 12 major cities and 2 major provinces. This year, FUSE plans to expand its affiliates to Calgary and Québec. The traditional means of teaching confines students to the four walls of the classroom, and fails to prepare students for a future in the real world of business. As one of the fastest-growing youth business organizations in Canada, we work to provide youth with relevant, hands-on training in various industries.

    Innovators, Questioners, and Risk Takers.

    The analytical, cognitive, and presentation skills each member gains are invaluable assets that are applicable to a countless range of careers. Whether they are tomorrow’s entrepreneurs, lawmakers, doctors, or engineers, our hope is that FUSE graduates will become change-makers. We are building the next generation of leaders.
